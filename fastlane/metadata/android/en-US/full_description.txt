<p><i>Aucards</i> is a free and open-source app where you can create colorful communication cards to help you communicate with others.</p>

<p>App is based on the concept of "autistic cards", which people with special needs use to quickly communicate their feelings, their requests or inform others of their conditions.</p>

<p><b>Highlights:</b></p>

<li>Create an endless amount of cards with unique messages</li>
<li>Choose any color from a rich palette – or create your own!</li>
<li>Organize your cards however you like: drag them around or add to favourites</li>
<li>Easy to use: just tap on the card to open it in fullscreen!</li>
<li>Bring your cards to any device: easily export and transfer them wherever you like</li>
<li>Completely free, open source and no ads – ever!</li>

<p><b>Who can benefit from this app:</b></p>

<li>People with autism/adhd</li>
<li>Children who have difficulties with verbal communication</li>
<li>Elderly people who need assistance</li>
<li>Or anyone in a situation where you can't speak: on a loud concert or a lecture!</li>
